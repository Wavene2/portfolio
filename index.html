Bone fracture detection

This project utilizes YOLO (You Only Look Once), a state-of-the-art object detection algorithm, to identify and classify bone fractures from medical images, such as X-rays or CT scans. The system is designed to provide a fast, accurate, and user-friendly tool for assisting healthcare professionals in diagnosing fractures and guiding treatment decisions.

The primary aim of the project is to:

  • Detect fractures in bone images with high precision.
  • Classify the fractures based on their type, location, or severity.
  • Provide a quick and accessible diagnostic tool for radiologists, orthopedic surgeons, and general practitioners.
